ABOUT OUR INSTITUTE

Government Engineering College, Bharuch is committed to providing quality technical education with state-of-the-art infrastructure and experienced faculty to nurture future engineers.

ADMISSION

Admissions are conducted through ACPC (Admission Committee for Professional Courses) Gujarat. Various undergraduate and postgraduate programs are offered in different engineering disciplines.

CAMPUS FACILITIES

Our campus is equipped with modern laboratories, well-stocked library, computer center, sports facilities, hostels, and all necessary amenities for a conducive learning environment.

MESSAGE FROM PRINCIPAL

As we step into another academic year, I reflect on the journey we've traversed and envision what lies ahead. Government Engineering College, Bharuch has been a cornerstone of engineering education in Gujarat for decades, building academic excellence, fostering innovation, and nurturing generations of engineers.

This institution represents more than just an educational facility; it is a beacon where dreams take shape and futures are forged. Our commitment to academic excellence has led to outstanding performance in various subjects, technical sports, and cultural events, and that showcase our holistic approach.

Our dedicated faculty and staff work tirelessly to ensure our students not only gain knowledge but also critical thinking skills necessary for professional success in the ever-evolving technological landscape. We understand that our contributions to the profession are provided, like intellectual contributions to society, through faculty and curricular events, and their provision that our students receive training that meet the trends of tomorrow.
