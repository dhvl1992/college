Principal's Message

Dear GEC Bharuch Family,

As we step into another academic year, I reflect on our journey with immense pride and optimism. Last year was a testament to our resilience, adaptability, and commitment to excellence. We celebrated significant milestones, including the NBA accreditation of our Mechanical and Civil Engineering departments, marking a leap toward global competitiveness.

This year, we build on our strengths and achievements. Our students' outstanding performances in Vibrant Gujarat, national sports, and cultural events, and their proactive role in social initiatives like aiding flood victims, exemplify the comprehensive education we provide. The intellectual contributions of our faculty, through patents, publications, and recognition on global platforms, continue to elevate our institute's stature.
