About Government Engineering College, Bharuch

GEC Bharuch Campus - A Hub of Engineering Excellence

Government Engineering College, Bharuch is one of eight New Government Engineering Colleges established in July 2004. It was established with the foresight to get talented engineers in the field of Electrical Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, Electronics & Communication Engineering, Civil Engineering and Chemical Engineering.

Our Heritage

The institute offers an outstanding learning environment with faculties dedicated to providing students global business capabilities required for success. The learning experience is influenced by the most recent developments in the field and delivered by experienced teachers and experts from various engineering disciplines.

Accreditation & Affiliation

The academic programmes are recognized by All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE) and the college is affiliated to Gujarat Technological University, Ahmedabad. It is administered by Education Department, Gujarat State.

"GEC Bharuch stands as a beacon of engineering education in Gujarat, fostering innovation and excellence in technical education since 2004."

Key Achievements

  • NBA Accreditation for Mechanical and Civil Engineering departments
  • AICTE approved programs across all engineering disciplines
  • Strong industry partnerships and placement records
  • Active research and development initiatives
  • Student achievements in national and international competitions

Campus Facilities

The college is equipped with state-of-the-art infrastructure including modern laboratories, well-stocked library, hostel facilities, sports complex, and various other amenities to support holistic development of students.
